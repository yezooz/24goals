How do we measure activity ?

- points for picking scores
- achieved trophies ie. picker of the month
- points for content added by user (below)

Points table:
1. submitted article +20 pts
2. submitted match analysis +10 pts
3. submitted video +5 pts
4. submitted image +3 pts
